Caesar Salad

Description

e

Caesar salad is such a classic. Want a crow-pleasing appetizer? Caesar salad.

Looking for a light meal? Caesar salad (plus your favorite protein).

Just want something quick and easy? Four-ingredient Caesar salad it is.

Buon Apetit!

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce.
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons.
  • Caesar dressing.

Steps

  1. Chop and wash your romaine lettuce.
  2. In a bowl, add your lettuce, croutons, dressing, and some shaved Parmesan cheese.
  3. Toss the salad to coat it in dressing and serve.
