Perfect Baked Potato

Description

This baked potato has a crisp, golden skin, and is light and fluffy on the inside - a perfect baked potato. Serve with a dollop of sour cream if you like. Great comfort food!

Buon Apetit!

Ingredients

  • A Big potato
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Your prefered tops

Steps

  1. Scrub and clean potato's skin.
  2. Pierce potato with a fork or knife.
  3. Coat potato with olive oil.
  4. Season it with salt.
  5. Bake in a 300 degree F oven until the potatoes are tender and golden brown, about 90 minutes.
  6. Top it with your prefered toppings
